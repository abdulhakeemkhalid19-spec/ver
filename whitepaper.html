01 — Abstract

$VER is a decentralized BEP-20 token on the BNB Smart Chain designed to reward early community members who help grow the ecosystem before its official exchange listing. With a total supply of 1,000,000,000 tokens and 20% allocated for community airdrop, $VER aims to create real value for participants through tasks, referrals, mining, and a post-listing fund of $50,000,000 USD.

"$VER exists to prove that a token's earliest supporters deserve the greatest rewards."

02 — Introduction

The cryptocurrency space has historically rewarded insiders and early investors over community members. $VER was created to change this dynamic by putting 20% of all tokens directly in the hands of the community before listing.

By completing simple tasks, referring friends, and participating in our mining phase, users can accumulate $VER tokens that will be distributed after our official exchange listing.

03 — Problem Statement

Unfair Token Distribution

Most cryptocurrency projects allocate the majority of tokens to founders, venture capital, and private investors — leaving the community with little opportunity to participate early.

Lack of Community Engagement

Projects often fail to build genuine communities before listing, resulting in low trading volume and rapid price decline after launch.

No Reward for Early Supporters

People who spread the word about a project before listing typically receive no direct compensation for their efforts.

04 — Our Solution

$VER solves these problems through a structured airdrop campaign that rewards community members for measurable contributions:

Task-Based Earning

Users earn $VER by completing social media tasks (following, retweeting, subscribing) that grow the $VER ecosystem organically.

Referral Program

A tiered referral system rewards users who bring new participants to the platform, with increasing bonuses as referral counts grow.

Phase 2 Mining

Daily mining allows registered users to earn additional $VER every 24 hours, creating ongoing engagement before the listing.

07 — Mining System

Phase 2 introduces a daily mining system that allows registered users to earn 50 $VER every 24 hours by actively engaging with the platform.

Mining is controlled by the admin and will be activated once the community reaches a critical mass of registered users. This ensures maximum impact when the mining phase begins.

Mining rewards are designed to maintain long-term engagement and ensure users stay active through to the listing date.

09 — Security

Account Security

All user accounts are protected using Firebase Authentication with Google and Twitter OAuth. No passwords are stored — all logins use secure OAuth flows or magic links.

Anti-Bot Measures

Our platform implements duplicate detection for emails, wallets, Twitter accounts, and Telegram accounts. Any account found using duplicate credentials will be flagged and disqualified from the airdrop.

Smart Contract Audit

Before token deployment, the $VER smart contract will undergo a full third-party security audit to ensure there are no vulnerabilities or backdoors.

Data Storage

All user data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore with row-level security rules ensuring users can only access their own data.

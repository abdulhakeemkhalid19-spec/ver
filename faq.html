Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about the $VER Airdrop, Ambassador Program, referrals, rewards and future listings.

What is $VER? +
$VER is a community-driven Web3 ecosystem designed to reward users through airdrops, referrals, ambassador campaigns and future blockchain utilities.
Is joining the airdrop free? +
Yes. Participation in the $VER airdrop is completely free.
When will rewards be distributed? +
Rewards will be distributed according to the official roadmap after campaign verification and token listing.
How do referrals work? +
Invite friends using your unique referral link. Eligible referrals increase your points and reward allocation.
How do I become an Ambassador? +
Visit the Ambassador page, complete the application form, and wait for approval from the $VER team.
Can I create multiple accounts? +
No. Multiple accounts are prohibited and may result in disqualification.
Which wallet should I use? +
Use a supported wallet compatible with the network announced by the $VER team. Never share your private keys or seed phrase.
When will $VER be listed? +
The listing date will be announced through our official website and social media channels after the roadmap milestones are completed.
How are Ambassador rewards calculated? +
Rewards are based on referrals, content quality, community engagement, consistency, and completed ambassador missions.
How can I contact support? +
Visit the Contact page on our website or reach out through our official Telegram community.
Is KYC required? +
If required for compliance or reward distribution, the team will announce it before the token distribution phase.
How can I avoid scams? +
Only trust links published on the official $VER website and official social media accounts. Never send funds or reveal your recovery phrase to anyone.
Can I change my wallet address later? +
If wallet updates are supported, instructions will be announced before token distribution.
Will there be more campaigns? +
Yes. Future campaigns may include staking, trading competitions, community events, and additional reward opportunities.
